About

Algorhythm generates rhythm sight reading exercises with correct engraving: beat groupings, beaming, tuplets, ties, swing markings. Practice with the metronome, subdivisions, count-in, play along, timeline view and a tunable drone.

Tap the round button to play or stop. Print gives clean sheets to read from.
